STATEMENT OF POLICY

DOST Region III assures every user of the website that every personal data shall be protected with the agency’s best ability. As what is stated in the Data Privacy Act of 2012, the agency will do its duty “to protect the fundamental human right of privacy of communication while ensuring free flow of information to promote innovation and growth”.

PRIVACY NOTICE

Personal Information
When you send your concerns or questions to us, the following personal information are being collected:
1. Your full name.
2. Your e-mail address.
3. Your address.
Use
Any information that the Deparment of Science and Technology Region 3 (region3.dost.gov.ph) will collect from its users shall be used primarily to give the site’s editors and administrators some useful data on the website usage and activities of users.
Should users be required to provide personal information such as name and email address (for subscriptions or correspondence), such information will only be used for the said purpose only. Rest assured that the information collected from users will not be used for marketing nor commercial purposes.

Kalusugan
Complementary Food Feeding Program
Project Details:

For 120 days, DOST-3 partners with BHWs and BNS who patiently prepared a nutritious complementary food blend every day for 985 children aged 6 to 36 months made of rice and legumes rich in protein. BHWs and BNS have also taken the initiative to make a variety of dishes using food blends such as pancakes, champorado, porridge, and mixing eggs and nutritious vegetables such as carrots and sweet potatoes to make it delightful and pleasent for the children.

In total, out of 985 underweight children, 684 of them graduated from a 120-day feeding program in which 50% of them had a normal weight. The weight gain recorded ranged from 8.57% to 22.90% in 120 days.

for more info visit: region3.dost.gov.ph

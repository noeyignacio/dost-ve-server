Kalusugan means health. This theme refers to projects that aid and benefit our healthcare system especially now in we're in the middle of a pandemic. Projects on this theme includes telemedicine device, and a health research and development consortia..
Central Luzon Health Research and Development Consortium (CLHRDC)
CLHRDC is one of the regional health research and development consortia (RHRDCs) organized by the Philippine Council for Health Research and Development (DOST-PCHRD) Expected to serve as the regional arm of the Philippine National Health Research System (PNHRS) and assist DOST-PCHRD in promoting health research culture, establishing mechanisms to strengthen health research capabilities, encourage collaboration and resource sharing between institutions, and promote the dissemination of regional/national health research agenda and utilization of health research results in the region.
CLHRDC’s mission is to strengthen the system of collaboration and enhance the research culture among member institutions towards improving the well-being of the people in Central Luzon

STARBOOKS

Project Details

STARBOOKS is stands for Science and Technology Academic and Research –Based Openly Operated Kiosk Station that contains thousand of digitized science and technology resources in various formats ( text and video/audio). These resources are placed in specially designed “pods” set in a user-friendly interface.

It aims to create in the field of science and technology which may increase the number of Filipinos enrolling S&T courses; encourage great and curious minds to develop new ideas - invention and innovation; and inspire one’s capacity for entrepreneurship and research.

Among its features:
- It is a standalone information kiosk, hence, information access is possible even without internet connection;
- The information are available only thru “read only” format.
- It is an S&T information resource were the materials are sourced out from local and foreign linkages , subscription, archive collections and in-house publications; and
- It has “Tamang DOSTkarte Livelihood videos” to promote and support entrepreneurial spirit.

Through the initiative of Regional Secretary MYRA M. ALIH, the DOST-ARMM in partnership with DOST-STII and with coordination with DepEd-ARMM the program is now on the smooth implementation by donating a complete package of STARBOOKS (including computer set) to the following schools identified and recommended by DepEd-ARMM:
1. Dumarpa Maniri Pimping National Science High School, Marawi City
2. ARMM Regional Science High School, Parang, Maguindanao
3Tawi-Tawi School of Arts and Trades (TTSAT) – STE Implementing School, Bongao, Tawi-Tawi
4. Lamitan National High School – STE Implementing School, Lamitan City
5. Jolo Agricultural School (JAS) – Senior Science High School Early Implementing school, Jolo, Sulu 6. For Basilan Province
7. Bacolod Kalawi National High School, Bacolod Lanao del sur.

Enabling Livelihood for PWD Group in Floridablanca, Pampanga

Project Details

The Florida Association of Persons with Disability (FAPWDs) continuously seek for a sustainable livelihood that will enable them to help provide for the needs of their family. As stated in Republic Act No. 7277 Sec. 40, Role of National Agencies and Local Government Units - Local government units shall promote the establishment of organizations of disabled persons in their respective territorial jurisdictions. National agencies and local government units may enter joint ventures with organizations or associations of persons with disabilities to explore livelihood opportunities and other undertakings that shall enhance their health, physical fitness, and the economic and social well-being. Relatively, one of the priority sectors of CEST is the PWDs. It is in response to the governmentâ€™s National Program on Poverty Alleviation.

The DOST-III through its Community Empowerment through Science and Technology (CEST) provided a training cum production to the members of the FAPWDs. Through this, the productivity of the PWDs was improved through the enhancement of their skills and abilities in handicrafts making specifically on bamboo-based materials.

The FAPWDs now have eight (8) members who are ready to start production of their bamboo-based handicraft products. The LGU on the other hand have committed to aid in the promotion of the bamboo-handicrafts to be produced by the FAPWDs.

Women in Action, Improving the Cashew industry in Conversion

Project Details

Nagkakaisang Kababaihan ng Conversion is an active women’s organization in Barangay Conversion, a geographically isolated barangay from the rest of the municipality of Pantabangan. The group was assisted with their cashew nuts business by providing them with training and processing equipment such as the following, double deck-oven, mechanical dryer, colloid mill, digital flatform balance, stainless steel table, multipurpose solar speed dryer, and product label design which resulted to better quality of their products and additional products realized, too, increasing their income by 23%. This spurred employment generation among the women in the community.

Through these interventions, the group’s challenges such as lack of knowledge and capability to process their products and low employment were addressed. Notable impacts for this project are improved product quality, improved productivity from 50% to 90%, and generated fourteen (14) employment. The group was also able to produce other products such as cashew butter. From May 2020 to June 2020 the group produced 129.15 kg (good quality) and 33.9 kg (rejects) of roasted cashew nuts with a total sale of Php 122,280.00 and produced five (5) 450 gram and two (2) 250 gram of cashew butter equivalent to Php 1,080.00. The group’s total sales became Php 123,360.00, 23% much higher compared to the previous year income of Php 100,000 after the intervention. In fact, the group was able to increase the marketability of their products through the labelling assistance provided by DOST-III.

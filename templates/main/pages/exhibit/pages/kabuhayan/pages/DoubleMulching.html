Double Mulching Technique (DMT)

Project Details

The Double Mulching Technique (DMT) is an adopted technology from the Philippine-Sino Center for Agricultural Technology (PhilSCAT) that uses low-cost materials instead of seedling trays for seedling preparation for mechanical transplanting. Unlike the traditional “dapog” method, DMT is an alternative seedling preparation method that uses mulches. The seedbeds are covered with holed plastic mulch which is topped with sieved garden soil. GI sheet frames are use as guides to secure the seedbed and to maintain an inch thick of garden soil. Once the seeds are sowed, the seedbed is covered with plastic mulch or net for a day to prevent pest infestation. Through this technique, it is expected that farmers will have greater yield as seedling stress will be avoided since the seedlings will not be pulled-out from the soil when transplanted unlike the traditional transplanting technique used by the farmers.

Empowering Women through Science and Technology

Project Details

The Samahang Ugnayan ng mga Kababaihang Mag-iisda ng Paltik is a women’s cooperative producing fish products like Sardines in oil, salmon in oil, dried fish tocino, fish longganisa and smoked fish. The group started producing in year 2016 with the initial capital of only Php 10,000.00. The cooperative was registered under business in year 2017 by the Department of Labor and employment with the enterprise registration number no. RO307-0917-WA-01. The primary resources of the group they use for production are yellowfin tuna, dorado/lamarang, and salmon. All of which can be available locally. The products are sold at the bagsakan center at Dingalan Bus Terminal and some are bought by the walk-in visitors of LGU-Dingalan.

The DOST-III improved the cooperative’s production through the provision of appropriate equipment such as Fish Dryer/Mechanical Dryers, Fish Meat Grinder, Vaccuum Packaging Machine, Chest Type Freezer, and other technology suitable for fish production. In addition to that, the agency also assisted on the improved packaging and label design execution.

Through these interventions, the group’s challenges such as lack of fish processing equipment and capability to process other fish products and low employment were addressed. Notable impacts for this project are improved product quality, improved productivity from 30% to 50%. The group was also able to produce other products and improved their fish cuttings. From May 2019 to May 2020 the group processed about 10MT of fish with a total sale of Php 150,000.00. The group’s total sales the interventions became Php 250,000.00 ,66% much higher compared to the previous year income. In fact, the group was able to increase the marketability of their products through the packaging and labelling assistance provided by DOST-III.

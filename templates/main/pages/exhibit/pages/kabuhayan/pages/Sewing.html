Rekindling the Sewing and Embroidery Industry of Bocaue

Project Details

With the help of Mayor Joni Villanueva, a group was identified to rekindle the weakened industry of sewing and embroidery of Bocaue. Bocaue Rivertown Wonders Multi-Products Sewers was established in 2017. Their main products are curtain makers, bedsheet and sofa cover makers, slippers makers, bag makers and designers. Despite having significant skills in sewing and embroidery, their main problem lies on the absence of the necessary equipment.

The DOST through the recommendation of Mayor Joni Villanueva, conducted an initial Technology Needs Assessment to identify the needed technology to make their production more efficient. Through this, the sewing and embroider industry in Bocaue have shown signs of life and is on its way to revival. The Bocaue Rivertown Wonders Multi-Products Sewers managed to produce an estimate of 5,000 pcs of face mask and 200 pcs of tshirt since the technological assistance was delivered.

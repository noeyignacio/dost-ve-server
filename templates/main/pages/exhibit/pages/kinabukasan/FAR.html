Far-UVC Gate

Kinabukasan

Far-UVC Gate

A novel technology for routine human decontamination in international and local transport gateways, infectious disease control, mitigation, and pandemic prevention.

Far-UVC is a special kind of UV

Far-UVC can kill microbes without harming human cells.

Far-UVC can’t penetrate biological material with a thickness of more than 1 micrometer (µm), such as human cells.

far-UVC can reach the genetic material of germs but not that within human tissue.

In general, viruses and bacteria have a size of less than 1 micrometer (µm).

With a precise dose, it is safe to use for germicidal purposes even in the presence of humans.

Unlike Conventional UV that causes Cataracts, far-UVC cannot go past tear layers.

Conventional UV can damage your skin, while Far-UVC cannot go past top layer of your skin.

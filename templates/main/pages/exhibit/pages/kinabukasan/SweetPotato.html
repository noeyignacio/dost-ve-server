Kinabukasan
Sweet Potato R&D Center
Click to watch.

The project aims to strengthen, increase and sustain the Sweet Potato-Clean Planting Material (SP-CPM) production efficiency and effectivity through science and technology and precision agriculture interventions. The project involves 3 components: Optimization of SP-CPM production protocol; Establishment of spatial information in Central Luzon; and Integrated crop management.

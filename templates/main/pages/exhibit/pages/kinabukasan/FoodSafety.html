Food Safety Control System for Street Food and Ambulant Vendors

Kinabukasan
Food Safety Control System for Street Food and Ambulant Vendors
Click to watch.

The project aims to establish Food Safety Control System Pilot Model through research-based assessment among selected food service establishments and ambulant street food vendors of Tarlac City. Through Tarlac State University’s Food Technology Department, profiling, environmental scanning and product sampling among respondents were conducted. The activities resulted to reliable data and information which will serve as basis for factual decision approach among policy making bodies of the LGU of Tarlac City towards its mandate of protecting the consumers’ health and welfare.
